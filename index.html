Breathe Deeply

Take a few minutes to focus on your breath. Inhale deeply through your nose, hold for a moment, and exhale slowly through your mouth. This practice can help calm your mind and reduce anxiety.

Engage in regular exercise, whether it’s a brisk walk, yoga, or dancing. Physical activity releases endorphins, which can improve your mood and alleviate stress.
